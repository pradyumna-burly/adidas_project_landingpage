Adidas Sales Analysis and Prediction

Insights and forecasting for Adidas sales data using advanced analytics.

Problem Statement

As part of my internship assignment at Technohacks Edutech, I executed this project under the mentorship of Sandip Gavit (Founder and CEO of Technohacks Edutech). This project aims to analyze Adidas sales data from 2020 and 2021, providing actionable insights into sales patterns and developing a predictive model to forecast future sales for optimal business strategy.

About Adidas

Adidas, a global leader in sportswear manufacturing, consistently pushes the boundaries of innovation. This project focuses on its footwear sales data, aiming to enhance strategic decisions through analytics.

Dataset Information

The dataset comprises detailed records of Adidas sales from 2020 and 2021. Sample columns include:

  • Retailer: e.g., Foot Locker
  • Invoice Date: e.g., 01-01-2020
  • Region, State, City
  • Product: e.g., Men's Street Footwear
  • Price per Unit, Units Sold, Total Sales
  • Operating Profit, Operating Margin
  • Sales Method: In-store, Outlet, Online
